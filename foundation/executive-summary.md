## Overview

**ISO Tracker** is the only evidence-based analysis platform dedicated exclusively to interstellar objects, helping people evaluate alien vs. natural claims through amateur-accessible frameworks. We combine real-time tracking, evidence-based analysis, and community discussion so anyone can meaningfully participate in humanity's search for alien life.

**Vision (25 years):** Build the definitive platform tracking every known interstellar object, with 10 million active users contributing observations, analysis, and discussion in humanity's search for alien life.

**Mission:** Dedicated exclusively to interstellar objects, we combine authoritative tracking, evidence-first discussion, and community collaboration so anyone can meaningfully participate in humanity's search for alien life.

## The Problem We Solve

**Customer Pain Points:**

Space enthusiasts face a fragmented, frustrating experience when trying to understand interstellar objects:

1. **Scattered Information:** Reddit threads offer speculation without rigor, Twitter provides fragmented expert takes, academic papers are inaccessible
2. **No Evidence Framework:** Can't distinguish credible analysis from speculation without PhD-level knowledge
3. **Event-Driven Frustration:** "Where is 3I/ATLAS right now? Is it really alien?" - no simple answer
4. **No Platform for Participation:** Want to evaluate claims themselves, not just read expert conclusions
5. **Diluted Astronomy Apps:** SkySafari shows 29 million celestial objects—finding ISOs is impossible

**The Gap:** No platform provides amateur-accessible evidence framework for evaluating ISO claims—either too speculative (social media) or too technical (academic sources).
