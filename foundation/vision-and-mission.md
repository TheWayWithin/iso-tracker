## LAUNCH MVP

**MVP Scope (AI-Assisted Solo Development):**

✅ **INCLUDES (Solo-Achievable)**:
- 2D sky map with 3I/ATLAS trajectory visualization
- Single data source integration (NASA Horizons API or similar)
- Simple timeline slider (past/future positions)
- Basic object database (planets, major stars, 3I/ATLAS)
- Embeddable "current ISO position" widget for backlinks and organic growth
- Journey-based pricing (Event Pass $4.99/month for Spectators, Evidence Analyst $19/month for Debaters)
- Web app + PWA (no app store approval delays)
- Community forum (Discord or similar, low-code solution)
- Email capture for event notification

❌ **EXCLUDES (Requires Team or Post-MVP)**:
- Basic AR overlay (deferred to Phase 2 to accelerate launch)
- True 3D visualization (too complex for solo dev)
- AR telescope integration (hardware partnerships needed)
- Multi-source data reconciliation (too time-intensive)
- Real-time discovery feed (requires infrastructure)
- AI speculation engine (ML expertise + compute costs)
- Mobile apps (App Store delays kill rapid launch)
- Advanced community features (complex backend)
- Institutional partnerships (requires business dev)

**Tech Stack (Solo-Friendly with AI Assistance):**
- Frontend: React + Three.js (basic) OR Babylon.js (simpler 3D if time permits)
- Backend: Firebase/Supabase (no-code backend)
- AR: A-Frame + AR.js (web-based AR, no native apps)
- Data: NASA Horizons API (free, simple)
- Hosting: Vercel/Netlify (free tier, instant deploy)
- Payments: Gumroad or Stripe Checkout (5 min setup)
- Community: Discord + Tally forms (no-code)

**Development Timeline:**
- **Phase 0**: Deploy pre-launch landing page with email capture (validation phase)
- **Phase 1**: Core tracking + news feed + debate dashboard + Discord community integration
- **Phase 2**: Subscription system + payment integration + embeddable widget
- **Phase 3**: In-app community (migrate from Discord to integrated discussion system)
- **Phase 4**: Advanced features (AR overlay, historical ISO archive, telescope integration)

## CONTENT & RETENTION STRATEGY

**The Challenge:**
ISOs are discovered every few years (1I/2017, 2I/2019, 3I/2024). The gap between discoveries is the single greatest retention threat.

**Strategic Approach:**
Build permanent value through evergreen content, educational engagement, and diversified user segments.

### Content Pillars (Ongoing Between Discoveries)

**1. Historical Deep Dives (Monthly Series)**
- **'Oumuamua Archive:** Interactive timeline of discovery, debate evolution, current scientific consensus
- **2I/Borisov Deep Dive:** Comparison with cometary behavior, what made it different
- **3I/ATLAS Legacy:** "What we learned" retrospective once observation window closes
- **Format:** Long-form articles + interactive 3D trajectory replays + community discussion threads
- **Goal:** Turn past ISOs into rich, searchable content that drives organic traffic

**2. Educational Modules (Weekly Releases)**
- **ISO Detection Science:** How astronomers identify interstellar vs. solar system objects
- **Trajectory Analysis 101:** Understanding hyperbolic orbits and eccentricity
- **SETI & Technosignatures:** The scientific search for extraterrestrial intelligence
- **Spectroscopy Explained:** How we determine composition from light
- **Format:** Bite-sized lessons (5-10 min reads) with quizzes and community challenges
- **Goal:** Build astronomy literacy, create habit of weekly engagement

**3. Data-Driven Stories (Bi-weekly)**
- "3I/ATLAS tracking: 6 months of data reveals..."
- "Community predictions vs. scientific consensus: Who was right?"
- "The most debated evidence: A retrospective"
- **Format:** Infographics + data visualizations + user contribution highlights
- **Goal:** Show users their participation matters, create social proof

**4. Expert Engagement (Monthly for Evidence Analyst Subscribers)**
- **Astronomer Q&A Sessions:** Live discussions with Avi Loeb, Karen Meech, other researchers
- **Paper Breakdowns:** Simplified summaries of new peer-reviewed ISO research
- **Prediction Challenges:** "What will the next ISO look like?" expert-guided speculation
- **Goal:** Maintain premium tier value between active ISO events

### Retention Mechanics

**Habit Loops:**
- **Weekly email digest:** "This week in interstellar science" (content + community highlights)
- **Monthly challenges:** "Predict the next ISO's characteristics" with leaderboards
- **Streak incentives:** "30-day learning streak" badges for consistent engagement
- **Anniversary notifications:** "One year ago, 3I/ATLAS was discovered - revisit the debate"

**Community Events:**
- **Quarterly retrospectives:** Community votes on "Most interesting finding of the quarter"
- **Historical debates:** "Re-litigate the 'Oumuamua debate with new evidence"
- **Citizen science projects:** Analyze historical data, contribute to open research

### Diversified Revenue Streams (Reduce Event Dependence)

**Education Segment:**
- **Classroom Plan ($99/year):** 30 student seats + teacher dashboard + curriculum guides
- **Target:** High school and university astronomy courses
- **Value:** Engage students in real scientific debate, track ISOs in real-time
- **Stability:** Academic year subscriptions provide predictable annual revenue

**Astronomy Club Segment:**
- **Club Plan ($149/year):** 50 member seats + custom club page + observation coordination tools
- **Target:** Amateur astronomy clubs, science enthusiast groups
- **Value:** Coordinate club observations, share findings, build community identity

- **Target:** Universities, planetariums, science museums
- **Value:** Use ISO Tracker data in research, education, public exhibits
