### 1. Was This Included in the PRD?

**No.**

The PRD (v2.0) mentions Avi Loeb and the "Scientific Context & Origin Debate" (Section 3), but it treats it as passive, educational content. This is a critical omission. The PRD describes *presenting* the debate; the Loeb Scale allows users to *participate* in it with a scientific framework.

### 2. What is The Loeb Scale?

Based on my research of the foundational paper on arXiv [1], the Loeb Scale is a 0-10 system designed to classify interstellar objects based on their anomalies and potential for being artificial.

**Key Features:**
*   **0-10 Scale:** Provides a clear, quantitative measure of an object's strangeness.
*   **Color-Coded Zones:** Green (Natural), Yellow (Anomalous), Orange (Suspected Tech), Red (Confirmed Tech).
*   **Specific Criteria:** Each level has clear, observable criteria (e.g., non-gravitational acceleration, unusual shape, spectral signatures).
*   **Data-Driven:** The scale is designed to be updated dynamically as new data comes in.

| Level | Category | Example Criteria | ISO Tracker Application |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0-1 | **Natural** | Consistent with known comets/asteroids. | **2I/Borisov** (Loeb Scale: 0) |
| 2-4 | **Anomalous** | Non-gravitational acceleration, unusual shape. | **1I/ʻ Oumuamua** (Loeb Scale: 4) |
| 5-7 | **Suspected Tech** | Maneuvers, non-natural signals, artificial heat. | The core of our monetization and retention strategy. |
| 8-10 | **Confirmed Tech** | Direct confirmation of artificial origin. | The ultimate payoff for our user base. |
